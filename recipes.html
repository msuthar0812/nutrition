Recipes

Lean Recipes:

Grilled Tofu and Vegetable Stir-Fry: Marinate tofu in a flavorful sauce and stir-fry with an assortment of colorful vegetables. Quinoa Salad with Chickpeas and Avocado: Combine cooked quinoa, chickpeas, diced avocado, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and a light lemon vinaigrette for a nutritious and filling salad. Baked Salmon with Steamed Broccoli: Season salmon fillets with herbs and bake until cooked through. Serve with steamed broccoli on the side.

Bulky Recipes:

Lentil and Vegetable Curry: Cook lentils with a variety of vegetables, spices, and coconut milk to create a hearty and flavorful curry. Sweet Potato and Black Bean Burritos: Fill whole-grain tortillas with roasted sweet potatoes, black beans, saut√©ed peppers and onions, and your favorite salsa. Turkey Meatballs with Whole Wheat Pasta: Prepare turkey meatballs using lean ground turkey, whole wheat breadcrumbs, and herbs. Serve with whole wheat pasta and marinara sauce.

Muscular Recipes:

Chickpea and Spinach Curry with Brown Rice: Make a protein-packed curry using chickpeas, spinach, tomatoes, and aromatic spices. Serve with brown rice for a complete meal. Grilled Chicken Breast with Quinoa and Roasted Vegetables: Season chicken breasts with herbs and grill until cooked. Serve with cooked quinoa and a medley of roasted vegetables. Beef Stir-Fry with Brown Rice: Stir-fry thinly sliced beef with an array of colorful vegetables and a savory sauce. Serve over brown rice.
